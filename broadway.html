Broadway shows are practically synonymous with New York City, and the word Broadway is often used as shorthand for theater itself. The best Broadway shows attract millions of people to enjoy the pinnacle of live entertainment in New York City. Visiting the Great White Way means attending one of 41 large theaters concentrated in the vicinity of Times Square, a majority of which seat more than 1,000 people.
